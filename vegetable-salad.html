Vegetable Salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce, chopped2 cups
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved1 cup
  • Cucumber, sliced1 pc
  • Carrots, julienned1/2 cup
  • Red onion, thinly sliced1/4 cup
  • Olive oil2 tbsp
  • Lemon juice1 tbsp
  • Salt and pepperto taste

Procedure

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly before preparing.
  2. Chop the lettuce and place it in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, carrots, and red onion.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  6. Serve immediately, chilled or at room temperature.
