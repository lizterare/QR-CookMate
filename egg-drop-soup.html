Egg Drop Soup

Ingredients

  • Chicken stock4 cups
  • Eggs3 pcs
  • Cornstarch2 tbsp
  • Water1/4 cup
  • SaltPinch
  • White or black pepperPinch
  • Spring onions2 tbsp

Procedure

  1. Pour 4 cups of chicken stock into a saucepan.
  2. Bring the stock to a gentle boil over medium heat.
  3. In a small bowl, dissolve 2 tablespoons cornstarch in 1/4 cup water.
  4. Slowly add the cornstarch mixture to the stock while stirring continuously until slightly thickened.
  5. Beat 2 eggs in a bowl.
  6. Slowly pour the beaten eggs into the simmering soup while stirring gently in a circular motion to create thin egg ribbons.
  7. Season with a pinch of salt and a pinch of pepper.
  8. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons chopped spring onions on top.
  9. Serve hot.
